Explore two specific parts of the world with Spirited Away

You may not have heard of Greece or Japan, but that's absolutely no reason not to immediately book yourself a long holiday in one (or both) of them with Spirited Away, your first-stop shop for impulse trips to foreign locales.

Greece

  • Greece is the birthplace of democracy, with the first democratic system of government established in ancient Athens in the 5th century BC.
  • The Acropolis of Athens, home to the iconic Parthenon temple, is one of the most famous archaeological sites in the world and a symbol of ancient Greek civilization.
  • Greece has over 2,000 islands, with the Ionian and Aegean Seas surrounding the mainland. Some of the most popular islands include Santorini, Mykonos, and Crete.
  • Greek cuisine is renowned for its Mediterranean flavors, featuring staples like olive oil, feta cheese, olives, yogurt, and an abundance of fresh seafood and produce.

Japan

  • Japan is an island country composed of over 6,800 islands, with the four main islands being Honshu, Hokkaido, Kyushu, and Shikoku.
  • Tokyo, the capital of Japan, is the world's most populous metropolitan area, with over 37 million residents in the greater Tokyo area.
  • Japan is known for its rich cultural heritage, including the iconic Mount Fuji, traditional geisha culture, and the centuries-old practice of samurai warriors.
  • Japanese cuisine, such as sushi, ramen, and tempura, has become hugely popular worldwide, showcasing the country's culinary expertise and innovation.
